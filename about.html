A little bit about me

My name is Naomi.I am pursuing a Bachelor's of Information Technology in my second year. This is my second year and as my first year was last year. Before Information Technology, I was a nursing major at Chattahoochee Technical College before I transfered here last year. My journey so far has been a life-changing and a really eye-opening experience as i have had the privilege and oppourtunity to experience the campus life while living on campus and having more access to resources, events, career fairs, and activities that I would not be able to experience outside of College. Kennesaw was the type of college that I had dreamed of going with a range of opportunites and a chance for students to blossom and make real life connections and bonding experiences to where I have come out my shell a little bit. I am 21 years old living on the Marietta Campus. I am Nigerian-american as my parents were born in Nigeria. I was born in Georgia and lived there all my life. I was actually born in Marietta and lived in Smyrna for some time. I grew up in a christian background, so i would consider myself a christian. I am very introverted and shy and enjoy and prefer spending time alone so I can recharge. I am slowly come out of my shell and making more friends and connections here on campus as I am also trying to find myself by exploring many different opportunities to pursue in the only time I have in life in college.
